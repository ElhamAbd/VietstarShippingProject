Our Story


35 Years of History

From its founding in 1987, Morning Star Travel & Tours has been dedicated to connecting families, friends, and cultures together. From the United States and Europe to the archipelago of the Philippines, Morning Star offered a wide variety of guided tours and travel packages to allow its customers to experience & explore the wonders of Asia, Europe, and North America.

However, like many other small businesses during the COVID-19 pandemic in late 2019, Morning Star found itself at a crossroads. Faced with a stalled tourism industry, the company decided to rebrand itself as VietStar Shipping & Travel to branch out into a new market. Fast forward to today, VietStar has not only maintained its close connection with their loyal customer base, but it also has reached out to new customers by providing a way to allow individuals to ship items to their friends and family in Vietnam.




Our Mission


Delivering Joy ~

No matter if its 10 miles or 10,000 miles, we here at VietStar understand the importance of family. Our goal is to help people connect with their loved ones back in Vietnam. Whether its gifts, mementos, souvenirs, or basic goods & necessities, we aim to offer a service that is one of the best in the industry. With our on-site inventory, customers can also purchase hard-to-find items right when they walk into the store!
